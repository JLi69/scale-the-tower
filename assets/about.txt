No hero has ever scaled the Tower of Doom
and survived to tell the tale.
However, perhaps you might be the first
to succeed at scaling and plundering
the tower...

CONTROLS
Left/Right Arrows or A/D: Move around
Up Arrow or W: Jump
Space: Attack
1/2: Sword/Bow
